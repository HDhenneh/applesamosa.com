About Zero Waste Group

At Zero Waste Group, we believe getting rid of unwanted items should be simple and stress-free. We are a small local business that cares about our community.

When you choose us, you're not just clearing space in your home—you're helping reduce waste that would usually end up in landfill. Our goal is to reuse and recycle as much as possible, so your items can have a second life instead of harming the environment.

We work hard to ensure your rubbish is handled the correct way, and we take pride in doing a great job for every customer.
