How it works

We'll show you the way.

Three steps from "I need a space" to "I found one."

01
Tell us what you need
Category, area, size, duration, and any specific requirements. The more specific, the better the match. Vague gets vague back.
02
Browse matched spaces
Curated listings filtered to your criteria. Not a firehose of irrelevant results. Real spaces with real availability and real contact details.
03
Book or enquire directly
No middleman markup. Book directly with the space owner or connect directly to enquire. Spacey Places facilitates the find, not the fee.

For Space Owners

Got a space sitting empty?

If you have unused commercial, leisure or storage space — list it. Spacey Places connects you with exactly the right kind of occupier for exactly the right duration.

No long-form portals. No agent fees for listing. Just a clean profile that shows up when someone searches for exactly what you have.

  • Free to list during the directory launch phase
  • Category, area, capacity, duration and access tags
  • Enquiry direct to you — no platform middleman
