# GULF or GOLF?
## The Premier Circuit — Tour 1 of 10

---

### HOW TO PLAY

Each tour is a full 18-hole round. Every hole presents 5 clues — your job is to guess whether each clue refers to:

🏌️ **GOLF** — A famous golf course, tournament venue, or golf-related term

🌊 **GULF** — A body of water named as a gulf

✅ **BOTH** — A place or term that applies to BOTH golf and gulfs

**Scoring per hole:**
- Get all 5 correct → **BIRDIE** (-1)
- Get 4 correct → **PAR** (0)
- Get 3 correct → **BOGEY** (+1)
- Get 2 or fewer → **DOUBLE BOGEY or WORSE** (+2+)

Your goal: Play all 18 holes and finish under par!
