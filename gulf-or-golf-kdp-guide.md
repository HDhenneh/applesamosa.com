---

### FORMATTING REQUIREMENTS

#### Margins
- Top: 0.75" (1.91 cm)
- Bottom: 0.75" (1.91 cm)
- Inside (gutter): 0.85" (2.16 cm) — **important for binding**
- Outside: 0.75" (1.91 cm)

#### Font
- Body: 10-12 pt (readable for puzzle book)
- Headings: 14-18 pt
- Font: Arial, Helvetica, or similar sans-serif (clean for puzzles)
- Or use a serif font for a classic book feel

#### Spacing
- Single spacing for puzzle tables
- 1.5 spacing for intro/answer explanations

---

### COVER SPECS

#### Paperback Cover Dimensions (6×9" book, 200 pages):

| Element | Size |
|---------|------|
| Full Cover (PDF/JPG) | 12.63" × 9.25" (32.08 cm × 23.5 cm) |
| Back Cover Width | 6.13" |
| Spine Width | 0.47" (varies by page count) |
| Front Cover Width | 6.13" |

**Formula:**
Spine = 0.002252 × page count + 0.060 (for white paper)

For 200 pages: 0.002252 × 200 + 0.060 = 0.51"

#### Cover Design Ideas

**Front:**
- Background gradient: lush green fairway fading to deep blue ocean
- Title (large, bold): **GULF or GOLF?**
- Subtitle: **Play 10 Full Tours — 18 Holes Each!**
- Visual: Golf ball on tee, with small wave ripples behind it
- Tagline: **The only puzzle book where every hole counts**

**Back:**
- Brief description of the game
- Sample puzzle snippet
- "900+ clues • 10 tours • Under par to win!"
- Barcode placeholder (KDP adds automatically)

**Spine:**
- Title: **GULF or GOLF?**
- Author name (if any)

### KDP UPLOAD STEPS

1. **Create interior** in Word/Google Docs
2. **Export to PDF** (ensure fonts embedded, proper margins)
3. **Create cover** in Canva/Photoshop — export as high-res JPG or PDF
4. **Go to kdp.amazon.com**
5. **Create new paperback**
6. **Enter details:**
   - Title: GULF or GOLF?
   - Subtitle: Play 10 Full Tours — 18 Holes Each!
   - Language: English
   - Author: [Your Name/Pen Name]
7. **Upload interior PDF**
8. **Upload cover file**
9. **Preview** — check for margin issues
10. **Set pricing**
    - Cost to print: ~$2.50-$3.50 (depends on B&W vs color)
    - Recommended list: $11.99-$14.99
    - Your royalty: ~$3.50-$6.00 at 60% royalty tier
11. **Publish!**

---

### COLOR VS BLACK & WHITE

| Option | Pros | Cons | Recommendation |
|---------|------|------|----------------|
| Black & White | Lower cost ($2-3/print) | Golf theme doesn't pop | Good for prototype, consider color for full release |
| Color | Vibrant, premium feel | Higher cost ($5-7/print) | Best for final version — golf courses deserve color! |
