# Market Signal: OpenClaw Setup Pricing

## Finding
People are charging **$1000** to set up OpenClaw for others.

## What This Means
1. **High-ticket demand exists** — Businesses are willing to pay for automation setup
2. **Service revenue stream** — We can offer setup/consulting alongside products
3. **Competition isn't price-sensitive** — Premium positioning works

## Opportunities
- "We'll set up OpenClaw for you" — £500-800 service
- "Automation audit" — Review existing setup, recommend improvements
- "OpenClaw training" — Teach teams to use it effectively
- Package with AI Cafe products for bundle deals

## Pricing Strategy Implications
Value IQ at £49 might be **too low** if market accepts £800+ for setup.
Consider:
- Pro tier: £99 (not £49)
- Agency license: £499/year (not £199)
- Setup package: £299 (includes onboarding + configuration)
